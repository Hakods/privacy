Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile applications published by Ahmet Hakan Altıparmak (“we”, “our”, or “the developer”). It explains what data our apps handle and how. Not every app uses every feature described below — an app only handles the data needed for the features you actually use.

Information our apps may handle

  • Camera, photos & microphone. Some apps let you take photos or record audio to use a feature (for example, completing a task or verifying an action). This content is used only to provide that feature and is stored on your device unless the feature explicitly requires sending it to our verification service. We do not sell your photos or use them for advertising.
  • App content & settings. Your in-app data, preferences, and progress are stored on your device.
  • Purchase data. Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. We receive whether your subscription is active — not your payment details.
  • Diagnostic identifiers. A device identifier may be used to attribute purchases and measure app performance. We do not collect your name, email, or contacts unless you email us directly.

Third-party services

  • Apple App Store / RevenueCat — subscription and purchase processing.
  • AI verification providers — where an app verifies a photo or input against a task, the content may be sent to a processing service solely for that check. Such content is not retained for training.

Data retention & deletion

Content and settings created in an app live on your device; deleting the app removes them. Where an app lets you delete individual items, you can do so at any time inside the app.

Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13 and do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date above reflects the current version.
